NCC

National Computing Contest(NCC) is held in PICT. It is organised by PICT-IEEE Student Branch(PISB) in collaboration with Nalini Foundation. The event is being organized since past few years and it is regarded as a reputed national level computing contest. In keeping with the tradition of bringing the best coders of the city under one roof, PISB is organizing NCC.

The contest comprises of several algorithmic problems of varying difficulty, which are to be solved by participants in C/C++. So, are you ready to analyse algorithms, search strings and traverse trees to climb your way up to victory?
